About Us

Freavian cleaners are a modern up-to-date cleaning company.It was established in 2018 and has a staff of approximately 25 members We are based in Nairobi Kenya and aim at creating cleaner homes conveniently.

Mision

We aim to bring cleaning solutions to everyday household in Kenya.Giving jobs and changing homes one at a time!

Vision

Be the leading cleaning solutions brand in East and Central Africa.
